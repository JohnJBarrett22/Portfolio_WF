About Me

I’m a Full Stack Developer, proficient in Python, Java and JavaScript. With 15+ years of proven, self sufficient problem solving experience in individual and collaborative environments...no challenge is too large. An early adopter of most technologies with strong interests in machine learning, virtual reality and cutting-edge web based applications.

Extensive, diverse and very successful background in retail leadership roles as a director of operations, district and store manager. Achieved extraordinary results in every position, consistently driving stores to top-tier performance rankings. Managed several high-volume, high-profile, and flagship locations with volumes up to $18.5M and teams of 130+.

Transitioned to tech, fulfilling a life long dream. While I excelled in an immersive coding program, my learning did not stop when I graduated. I continue to hone my skills and branch into other technologies through online learning resources, building out personal projects, volunteer efforts, open source contributions and freelancing. My technical skills include:

  • LANGUAGES: Python, Java, TypeScript, JavaScript, CSS3, HTML5
  • FRAMEWORKS: Angular, Django, Flask, Spring Boot, Express, Materialize, Foundation, Bootstrap
  • SERVERS: Node.js, Apache Tomcat, Nginx, Gunicorn, PM2
  • DATABASES: MySQL, SQLite, MongoDB
  • LIBRARIES: jQuery, Socket.io, Mongoose, Phaser
  • DEVELOPMENT TOOLS: VSC, Spring Tool Suite, Postman, SQL Workbench, DB Browser, Git, GitHub
  • CLOUD COMPUTING: EC2, S3, SES, AWS Lambda
  • METHODOLOGIES: AJAX, CRUD, RESTful APIs, MVC, MVT, Agile
  • SOFTWARE: Webflow, Adobe Photoshop
